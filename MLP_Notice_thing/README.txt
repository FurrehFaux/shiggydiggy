This little joke page used to be the main page of a series of websites ( whoov.es, doctorcolage.com, google.com.z.love.and.tolerance.the.wonderbolts.com ) which have been taken down.
After the take-down, I recreated (to the best of my ability) the joke page.

For this script to display properly, you need to have at least PHP 5 to install. (or you could just take out the PHP scripts in the document, and replace them with static text.)
